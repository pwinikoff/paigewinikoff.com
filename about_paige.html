History of Community Service

Las Lomitas Elementary School Board - Paige currently serves on the Board of Trustees and is finishing her second year of a two year term. In addition, she serves on the Classified Staff Employee Association Committee and the Community Advisory Committee.

Las Lomitas Education Foundation Board - Paige served two years on the Las Lomitas Education Foundation Board and then convinced her husband to serve two years afterward. She helped chair the auction, and raised significant funds to support Las Lomitas.

ABC Readers/Project Cornerstone - Paige led monthly classes in 4th and 5th grades for three years, using children's literature to share concepts of respect, empathy, integrity, equality and social justice, honesty, responsibility and kindness.

La Entrada Middle School - Paige filled in as a substitute teacher during shortages.

Las Lomitas Parent-Teachers Association - Paige served as Room Parent, organized parties, and facilitated communication between teachers and parents. Other volunteer roles included Kindergarten Round-Up, library helper.

Menlo-Atherton Foundation for the Future - Paige served as Campaign Chair for the M-A FFF, helping to raise about 2.5 million dollars for the high school.

United States Naval Sea Cadet Corps/Band of the West - Paige is currently an Ensign with the USNSCC, a youth group honoring veterans and those who serve with their music. She is the recruitment officer for the unit.

Menlo-Atherton High School PTA - Paige served as Co-President of the PTA between 2022 and 2024. Her proudest achievements in that role include the creation of a new English Language Conversation Committee for students new to the United States; a "Prom Closet" collecting hundreds of dresses, suits and grants from local retailers, serving over 300 students so far; creation of a Bike Safety Committee; organizing Parent Social Committees for each of the four classes at the high school; and successful forums for community engagement with law enforcement after two loaded guns were found on campus.

Education and Professional Experience

Paige graduated UC Berkeley with dual degrees before attending the American University, Washington College of Law. Afterward, Paige studied at Pardes Institute of Jewish Studies in Jerusalem. She is a current member of the California State Bar and practices in the area of employment law.

Achievements while serving on the Las Lomitas School Board

Paige has worked tirelessly to promote academic excellence for every student within the Las Lomitas School District, concentrating on bringing academic rigor back to pre-Covid levels and beyond. She currently serves on the CSEA bargaining committee and the Community Advisory Committee. Paige recognizes the value of public land and green space and has worked to restore access to field and playground for constituents living in Ladera. If re-elected, Paige hopes to continue her work focusing on Academic Excellence, Teacher Support, and Community Empowerment.
